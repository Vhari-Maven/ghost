# Jimmy's Gym Equipment Catalog

A reference of what's available at the gym, with photos in `static/equipment/`.

---

## Cardio

### Treadmills
`treadmills.jpg` — Row of **Precor treadmills** with built-in screens along the windows. Adjustable speed and incline for walking or running.

### Peloton Bikes
`peloton-bikes.jpg` — **Peloton stationary bikes** with large touchscreens for streaming classes. Clip-in pedals, adjustable seat and handlebars.

### StairMaster
`stairmaster.jpg` — Rotating staircase machine for intense cardio and leg work. Handles for stability, digital display for tracking.

### Ellipticals
`ellipticals.jpg` — **Precor elliptical machines** for low-impact cardio. Moving handles engage upper body too.

### Assault AirRunner
`assault-runner.jpg` — Curved, self-powered treadmill — the harder you push, the faster it goes. There's also an **Assault AirBike** nearby.

## Strength Machines (Precor Line)

### Chest Press
`chest-press-machine.jpg` — Seated machine for pushing movements. Targets chest, front shoulders, and triceps. Adjustable seat, weight stack on the side.

### Leg Extension / Leg Curl (Combo)
`leg-extension-curl-machine.jpg` — **One machine, two exercises.** For extensions: sit facing forward, extend legs. For curls: adjust position, curl heels back toward you.

### Lat Pulldown
`lat-pulldown-machine.jpg` — Pull the bar down to work lats and biceps. Wide grip bar attached.

### Seated Leg Press
`leg-press-machine.jpg` — Seated machine for pressing weight away with your legs. Works quads, glutes, and calves. Can also use for calf raises by placing balls of feet on lower edge.

### Assisted Pull-up / Dip Machine
`assisted-pullup-machine.jpg` — Knee platform provides counterweight to make pull-ups and dips easier. Great for building up to bodyweight versions.

### Functional Trainer (Cable Machine)
`cable-machine.jpg` — **Precor FTS** — dual adjustable pulleys. Super versatile: rows, presses, flies, face pulls, core work, bicep curls, tricep pushdowns... basically everything. Various handles and attachments on the side.

## Free Weights

### Adjustable Bench
`adjustable-bench.jpg` — Can be set flat, incline, or decline for dumbbell and barbell work.

### Flat Bench + Plates
`flat-bench-plates.jpg` — Flat bench with bumper plates and Olympic barbell. Color-coded plates: red, yellow, green, blue.

### Power Rack / Platform
`power-rack-platform.jpg` — Full power rack with Olympic lifting platform (wooden inlay). Good for squats, bench, deadlifts, Olympic lifts. Bumper plates stored on rack.

### Half Rack
`half-rack.jpg` — Smaller rack with safety bars for catching failed lifts. Mirror behind for form checks.

### Fixed Barbell Rack
`barbell-rack.jpg` — Pre-weighted barbells from ~20-110 lbs. Grab and go without loading plates.

### Dumbbell Racks
`dumbbell-racks.jpg` — Two large racks of rubber-coated hex dumbbells in various weights. Hex shape prevents rolling.

## Bodyweight & Accessories

### Captain's Chair
`captain-chair.jpg` — Hang from the arm rests, lift knees or legs for core work. Also has handles for dips.

### Kettlebells & Medicine Balls
`kettlebells-medballs.jpg` — Shelves with kettlebells, slam balls, wall balls, and a stability ball. Good for functional training.
